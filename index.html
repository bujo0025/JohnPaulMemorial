Early Life

Paul was born John Paul Raymond Von Kurnatowski Barrett II on July 11, 2005. He was born Rochester, New York, a beautiful city off the shore of Lake Ontario in upstate New York. In his early years, his family moved to Toronto, Cananda where Paul lived as he grew up. It is here where he discovered his love for dance and musical theatre. Paul strived at school in acedemics, athletics, and the performing arts. In 2019, when he graduated elementary school he was awarded the triple crown; the Outstanding Athlete Award, the Cameron R. Macinroy Music Award, and the coveted Valedictorian award. Paul was seen by many as a child genius, being fluent in English, French, and Italian, not to mention mastering instruments like the violin and the piano.

High School

Paul entered high school with impecable knowledge in Mathematics, Biology, World History, and Physics. Grades 9 through 12, Paul was awarded the Cole Capatini Gallacher Award, more simply known as the Outstanding Student Award. Paul eased through high school, boasting an impressive 4.0 GPA across all 4 years. Paul was an active member in his community and participated in a plethora of school clubs and sports. He helped lead his high school to the OFSAA mens finals for Lacrosse in both his 3rd and 4th year. They won both years. Paul was recognized as an All-American rower, competing in rowing across Canada and the United States, even recieving a full ride scholarship to the University of Victoria in British Columbia where he was set to compete for 4 years in rowing whilst completing his degree in Astrophysics.

A Tremendous Life

After high school, Paul attended U-Victoria for one year where he excelled in both rowing, and his respective program. He made the deans list with a 3.8 GPA and won multiple rowing meets across the Pacific Coast. In the summer of 2024, he proposed to his high school sweetheart Nhẫn kim Wilson. Paul was set to begin an internship this February at the Canadian Space Agency where he would work in a student position for his entire second year of University. He and Nhẫn kim were set to wed in the Spring of 2026 after a year of work at the CSA. Paul has lived a thousand lives. He mastered linguistics, sports, instruments, academics, and arts. In many ways, it seemed as though Paul had mastered life, a true child prodigy turned genius adult
